GANACHE was created in 2026 by Michael Fernandez.

GANACHE is a body care brand inspired by sweet desserts and comforting scents. Our goal is to create body washes that make people feel fresh, confident, and smell sweeter throughout the day.

Each scent is inspired by delicious treats like chocolate, vanilla, and tropical drinks, bringing fun and unique fragrances to your daily routine.
